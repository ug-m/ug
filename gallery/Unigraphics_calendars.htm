The Unigraphics Calendars

The Unigraphics Calendar has been a great opportunity for Unigraphics customers to showcase their products as well as a forum for exhibiting the capabilities of UG/Photo and the artistic skills of individual users.  Below are images of the front and back cover of each years calendar.

1998 Unigraphics Calendar

The 1998 Calendar was unique in that it never really had a company name on it (true there was a reference to EDS in the copyright block, but that was all).  The thing was that the company knew that they were being spun off from EDS after the first of the year and that there was going to be an IPO and a new name, the problem was that the calendar was going to be coming out at the end of 1997 and they did not want to have to reprint the calendar half-way through the new year so it was decided to not have the company name displayed anywhere (except as noted above).  Therefore there is no "EDS Unigraphics" displayed as in years past and of course since the name "Unigraphics Solutions" had not yet been decided upon so that couldn't be used either, only the name Unigraphics was displayed on the front and rear covers.
