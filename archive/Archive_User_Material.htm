Archive of User Directed Material

Here in the Archive of User Directed Material you will find many documents and other printed material that was either published by the Unigraphics Users Group or by the Unigraphics organization generally for the purpose of providing information to existing customers.  Example of these include newsletters, product updates and other similar material.  While many people may consider some of the examples included here as sales or marketing material, the reason they are in this section is because they were not generally provided to non-users of Unigraphics.  Most of these will have full-size, readable versions available for you to examine (just select the image of the item).  The items are placed in roughly chronological order of their publication.
