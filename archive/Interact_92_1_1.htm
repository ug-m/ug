Interact, Volume 92 - Number 1, February, 1992

This issue reviewed the upcoming 16th Annual Unigraphics Users Meeting to be held in Long Beach, CA.  The issue was of particular importance since it was the first time that many customers had had an opportunity to hear details of the recent acquisition of the Unigraphics organization by EDS.  There were interviews with both John Mazzola, President of the Unigraphics division of EDS and Dennis Drislane, Vice President of Operations of the business unit that the Unigraphics organization was going to be part of and was also acting as the EDS executive responsible for the transition process.
