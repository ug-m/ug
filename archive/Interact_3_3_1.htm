Interact, Volume 3 - Number 3, May, 1983

First we have to explain the that the above volume number is correct.  You see there was a decision made to change the numbering scheme so the volume number would sync with the last digit of the year.  This issue gave a run down on the events from the 7th Annual Unigraphics Users Meeting held in Costa Mesa, CA in February.  The issue included the minutes from the business meeting and the various special interest sessions.  There was also a listing of the issues and responses from the Q&A sessions.
