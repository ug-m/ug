Interact, Volume 90 - Number 1, February/March, 1990

With the new decade there was again the need to come up with a new numbering scheme for the Interact (apparently no one had thought that they would still be publishing in 1990 when they came up with the last single digit numbering scheme).  This issue was provided to the attendees of the 14th Annual Unigraphics Users Meeting held in Long Beach, CA.  The issue included the agenda for the sessions as well as the biographies of the presenters.
