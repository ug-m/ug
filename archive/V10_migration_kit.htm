V10 Migration Kit, August 1994

This Migration Kit consisted of several manuals and head start guides as well information on training schedules and a response card where more information could be requested.  Note that this kit was sent to all customers who were upgrading from V9 to V10.  The first kits were sent with the V10.3 shipment as that was the first release of Unigraphics V10 that was sent to all customers.
