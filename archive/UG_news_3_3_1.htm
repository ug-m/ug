UG User News, Volume 3 - Number 3, Fall, 2001

This was the first issue since the announced acquisition of SDRC by UGS and that was the cover story.   There was also an update on the activities from the National Users Group Meeting in Orlando as well as several of the Regional User Group meetings.  There were also several articles covering certain aspects of Unigraphics such as GD&T, Assemblies, a recent CAE JAD and a update on the transition from GRIP NC to API CAM.
